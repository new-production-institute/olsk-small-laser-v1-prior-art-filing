# OLSK Small Laser v1 filing repository

This repository serves as the pre-emptive prior-art filing for the **OLSK Small Laser v1**

It supports the work presented in the WIP paper:

> **“Increasing the Visibility of Open-Source Projects as Prior Art in Patent Assessments.”**

Its purpose is to document the OLSK Small Laser to ensure its status as accessible, verifiable prior art.

## Relation to Upstream Project
This is a **fork** of the original open-source project:

**https://github.com/Open-Lab-Starter-Kit/OLSK-Small-Laser**

The fork is focused solely on the **OLSK Small Laser v1**, preserving relevant artifacts for prior-art verification and citation within research and patent review contexts.

## Purpose of This Repository

- Establish clear prior art for OLSK Small Laser v1.
- Support transparency for protective patent assessments.
- Provide a stable, citable reference accompanying the above-mentioned research paper or other research papers.
- Maintain a focused, version-specific archival snapshot separate from ongoing upstream development.
